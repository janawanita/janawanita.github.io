Ensemble

Ensemble is a concept mobile application that connects local musicians with similar or different musical styles, depending on the userâ€™s specifications. It provides a platform that allows the user to easily connect and collaborate with other musicians in their area.

Personas & Moodboard

Before beginning the design process, it was important for me to create a moodboard and personas that represented the target audience for Ensemble. The users of the app would be musicians who are struggling to meet people to collaborate with. They are social and enjoy connecting with others especially through sharing a common interest such as music. The moodboard demonstrated the atmosphere and feel that I wanted the app to express. Both these deliverables allowed me to keep the target audience in mind as I began the design process.

App Screens

Onboarding Flow

Simple onboarding screens that help guide the user and introduce them to the feel and purpose of the app. The user can begin by either logging in or signing up for an account.

Personalizing Account & Discovering

Once the user creates an account, they are able to personalize their profile by having the option to add content such as videos, audio files and photos. They can discover local musicians by pressing the magnifying glass icon which directs them to the discover page. Here, users can enter their location, the type of musicians they are looking for and their preferred genres. This will narrow down their potential matches.

Finding Matches

Once the user finishes entering their preferences, a list of musicians within their locality matching their specifications will be displayed. The user may scroll through the list and view the profiles of their potential matches. They can add others as friends to view their content and send personal messages.
