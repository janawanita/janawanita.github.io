Ensemble

Designing a Concept Mobile App that Connects Local Musicians

Overview

About Ensemble

Ensemble is a concept mobile application that connects local musicians based on the user’s specifications. It provides a platform that allows the user to easily connect and collaborate with other musicians in their area. I created this design solution as a way to solve the problem of musicians such as myself, having difficulty meeting others to collaborate with.

Personas

Understanding the user

Before beginning the design process, it was important for me to create a moodboard and personas that represented the target audience for Ensemble. The users of the app would be musicians who are struggling to meet people to collaborate with. They are social and enjoy connecting with others especially through sharing a common interest such as music. I interviewed students in my unviersity who fit the target audience of the app and developed the following personas. The moodboard demonstrated the atmosphere and feel that I wanted the app to express. Both these deliverables allowed me to keep the target audience in mind as I began the design process.




Ideation

Creating solutions to tackle user painpoints

In order to solve painpoints such as having difficulty meeting people with a certain musical style or people who play a certain instrument, I came up with the following app features that would make the process of meeting other muscians as effective as possible.

  1. Personal profile that lets the user post videos and audio of their work as well as specify which instruments they play.
  2. Ability to select location, preferred instruments and genres when discovering local musicians.
  3. Ability to view profiles & send friend requests to the potential musicans the app matches users with.
  4. Messaging feature that allows users to communicate and set up meeting times.

App Screens

Understanding the flow of the app

Onboarding Flow

Simple onboarding screens that help guide the user and introduce them to the feel and purpose of the app. The user can begin by either logging in or signing up for an account.

Personalizing Account & Discovering

Once the user creates an account, they are able to personalize their profile by having the option to add content such as videos, audio files and photos. They can discover local musicians by pressing the magnifying glass icon which directs them to the discover page. Here, users can enter their location, the type of musicians they are looking for and their preferred genres. This will narrow down their potential matches.

Finding Matches

Once the user finishes entering their preferences, a list of musicians within their locality matching their specifications will be displayed. The user may scroll through the list and view the profiles of their potential matches. They can add others as friends to view their content and send personal messages.
