A little about me

I began my journey into UX/UI design after teaching myself the basics of front-end web development. As I was learning how to code simple websites, I realized my passion lied in the design aspect of these projects and soon discovered the field of UX/UI design.

I attended classes at George Brown College and used online sources to learn the basic principles of UX/UI design. Quickly, I realized how much I enjoyed the content and was drawn in by the perfect combination of critical thinking and creativity. I combined my front-end web development skills and newly obtained knowledge in UX/UI design to build my portfolio and continue to do so.

Apart from design, my hobbies include playing the guitar, snowboarding & hanging out with my friends.
