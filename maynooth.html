Maynooth Furniture

Maynooth Furniture is a business offering affordable high-end furniture. My objective was to design an e-commerce website for people to browse and purchase furniture for home delivery. I created a prototype for both web and mobile versions of the site.

Persona & Moodboard

I started with creating a moodboard and persona in order to have an understanding of the target audience for Maynooth Furniture and an idea of what the website would look like. The users of the website and app would be middle-high class individuals with a taste for high-end but affordable furniture. Both these deliverables allowed me to keep the target audience in mind as I began the design process.
